Ramen

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Pork bones - usually leg bones or neck bones
  • Noodles - specialized ramen noodles
  • Pork slices - like pork belly or pork loin
  • Vegetables - such as bok choy, spinach, green onions, etc.
  • Aromatics - like minced garlic, minced ginger, etc.
  • Seasonings - such as soy sauce, miso, salt, sugar, etc.
  • Garnishes - like nori, egg (soft boiled or hard boiled), fish cake, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, bean sprouts, etc.
  • Chicken bones - to enhance the depth of the broth (optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Clean the pork bones and place them in a large pot filled with enough water.
  2. Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to medium-low and simmer for 1-2 hours, skimming off any foam that forms on the surface.
  3. Add chicken bones (if using), aromatics, and seasonings, and continue to simmer for another 1-2 hours, until the broth turns milky white and thickens.
  4. Cook the noodles in a separate pot according to package instructions or until done.
  5. Drain the noodles and place them in a bowl.
  6. Ladle the pork bone broth over the noodles, then add the pork slices and your desired vegetables and garnishes.
  7. Garnish with green onions and other condiments like chili oil or soy sauce, adjusting to taste.
  8. Enjoy your piping hot bowl of Japanese Tonkotsu Ramen!
