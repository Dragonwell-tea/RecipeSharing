Macarons

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 cup almond flour
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 large egg whites (room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • Food coloring (optional)
  • Filling of your choice (e.g. buttercream, chocolate ganache, jam)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Sift the almond flour and powdered sugar together to remove any lumps.
  2. In a clean bowl, whisk the egg whites until they become foamy. Then, gradually add granulated sugar and cream of tartar, continuing to whisk until stiff peaks form.
  3. Add your chosen food coloring and gently fold it into the meringue.
  4. Fold the sifted almond flour and powdered sugar into the meringue in two or three batches. Be careful not to overmix; the batter should flow like lava.
  5. Transfer the batter to a piping bag with a round tip and pipe onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Let the macarons sit at room temperature for 30-60 minutes to form a skin.
  7. Preheat the oven to 300°F (150°C) and bake the macarons for 15-20 minutes.
  8. Let them cool completely on the baking sheet.
  9. Once cooled, fill the macarons with your chosen filling.
  10. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for at least 24 hours before enjoying.
