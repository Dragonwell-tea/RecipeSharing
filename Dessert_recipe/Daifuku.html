Daifuku

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Glutinous rice flour (Mochiko)
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch or potato starch (for dusting)
  • Anko (sweet red bean paste, either smooth or chunky)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: simple
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Mix glutinous rice flour, sugar, and water in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and microwave it for a few minutes, stirring at intervals, until the mixture becomes opaque and sticky.
  3. Dust a clean work surface with cornstarch or potato starch to prevent sticking.
  4. Transfer the sticky rice mixture onto the dusted surface and let it cool slightly.
  5. Once cool enough to handle, divide the dough into small balls.
  6. Flatten each ball and place a small amount of anko in the center.
  7. Fold and seal the mochi dough around the anko to form a ball.
  8. Dust with more cornstarch or potato starch to prevent sticking.
  9. Enjoy your Daifuku!
