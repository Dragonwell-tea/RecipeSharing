mousse

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Sponge cake or biscuit base
  • Heavy cream
  • Gelatin or agar-agar
  • Sugar
  • Vanilla extract (or other flavorings)
  • Chocolate or fruit puree (optional, for flavoring)
  • Fresh fruit (for garnish, optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: difficult
    • Required time: 30min

Steps

  1. Prepare the sponge cake or biscuit base and place it at the bottom of a cake mold.
  2. Soften gelatin in cold water (or prepare agar-agar as per instructions).
  3. Whip the heavy cream with sugar and vanilla extract until soft peaks form.
  4. If using, fold in melted chocolate or fruit puree into the whipped cream.
  5. Melt the gelatin and mix it into the mousse mixture until well combined.
  6. Pour the mousse over the sponge base in the mold.
  7. Refrigerate for several hours until the mousse is set.
  8. Once set, remove from the mold and garnish with fresh fruit or chocolate shavings if desired.
