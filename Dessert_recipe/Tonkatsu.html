Tonkatsu

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Pork cutlets (thinly sliced)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Flour
  • Eggs (beaten)
  • Bread crumbs (Panko)
  • Oil (for frying)
  • White rice
  • Tonkatsu sauce or Japanese curry sauce (optional, for serving)
  • Lettuce or cabbage (shredded, for garnish)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Season the pork cutlets with salt and pepper.
  2. Lightly coat the pork cutlets with flour.
  3. Dip the cutlets into the beaten eggs, ensuring they're fully coated.
  4. Place the cutlets in the bread crumbs, making sure they're fully covered.
  5. Heat the oil in a pan, and when it's hot enough, fry the cutlets.
  6. Remove the cutlets when they're golden brown and place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  7. Slice the cutlets into strips and serve them on a plate with rice.
  8. Optionally, drizzle with tonkatsu sauce or Japanese curry sauce for added flavor.
  9. Garnish with lettuce or shredded cabbage.
