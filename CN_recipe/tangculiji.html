Sweet&Sour Pork

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 300g Pork tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 1 Egg, beaten
  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup Pineapple chunks
  • 1/3 cup Bell peppers (red, green, or yellow), diced
  • 1/3 cup Onion, diced
  • 3 tbsp Ketchup
  • 2 tbsp Vinegar

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Marinate the pork tenderloin slices with salt and set aside for 10 minutes.
  2. Dip each slice of marinated pork into the beaten egg, then coat thoroughly with cornstarch.
  3. Heat oil in a frying pan or wok over medium-high heat. Once hot, fry the pork slices until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.
  4. In the same pan, reduce to medium heat and saut√© onions and bell peppers until slightly tender.
  5. Add pineapple chunks and stir briefly.
  6. Combine ketchup, vinegar, sugar, and soy sauce in a bowl. Mix well and pour into the pan.
  7. Bring the sauce to a simmer and then add the fried pork slices. Toss well to ensure each piece is coated with the sauce.
  8. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until everything is heated through and well combined.
  9. Transfer to a serving plate and serve immediately with steamed rice or noodles.
