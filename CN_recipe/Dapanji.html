BigPlate Chicken

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 500g Chicken thighs or drumsticks
  • 200g Hand-pulled noodles (or wide flat noodles)
  • 2 Potatoes, sliced into thin wedges
  • 3 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 Onion, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsp Sugar
  • 1 cup Chicken broth or water
  • 2-3 dried Red chili peppers

    Other

    • Difficulty level: difficult
    • Required time: 30min

Steps

  1. In a wok or large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and brown on all sides. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same wok, saut√© the onions and garlic until they are soft and fragrant.
  3. Return the chicken to the wok and add soy sauces, sugar, star anise, cinnamon, bay leaves, Szechuan peppercorns, dried chilies, and cumin seeds. Stir-fry for a couple of minutes.
  4. Add the chicken broth or water, ensuring the chicken is almost covered. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to medium-low and simmer for about 20 minutes.
  5. Add the potato wedges to the wok and continue simmering until both the chicken and potatoes are fully cooked.
  6. In a separate pot, cook the noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and add them to the wok, stirring gently to coat the noodles in the sauce.
  7. Transfer to a large plate or serving dish. Garnish with chopped coriander and green onions.
  8. Serve hot and enjoy!
