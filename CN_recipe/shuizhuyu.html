Spicy Boiled Fish

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 500g Fish fillets (like tilapia or cod), thinly sliced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 5 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 2 Green onions, chopped
  • 1 cup Bean sprouts
  • 15-20 Dried red chili peppers
  • 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns
  • 4 cups Chicken or fish broth
  • 3 tbsp Chili bean paste (Doubanjiang)
  • 2 tbsp Light soy sauce

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Marinate the fish slices with salt and a bit of the starch slurry for about 10 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, bring the chicken or fish broth to a simmer.
  3. While the broth simmers, heat oil in a separate wok or skillet over medium heat. Add dried red chili peppers and Sichuan peppercorns. Fry until fragrant.
  4. Add in the minced ginger, garlic, and chili bean paste, stirring frequently until the oil is red and fragrant.
  5. Transfer the spicy oil mixture into the simmering broth. Add light soy sauce and sugar, stirring well.
  6. Gently slide the marinated fish slices into the spicy broth. Let them cook until they turn white and are just cooked through.
  7. Add the bean sprouts and cook for another 2 minutes.
  8. Thicken the broth slightly with the remaining starch slurry, stirring continuously.
  9. Transfer the fish and broth to a serving bowl, and garnish with chopped green onions.
  10. Serve hot and enjoy the numbing and spicy flavors!
