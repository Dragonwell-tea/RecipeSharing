KungPao

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken breast, diced
  • 1/2 cup Unsalted roasted peanuts
  • 8-10 Dried red chilies
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1-inch Ginger, minced
  • 2 Green onions, chopped
  • 2 tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Chinese black vinegar
  • 1 tsp Sugar

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 15min

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with cornstarch and 1 tbsp of light soy sauce for 15 minutes.
  2. In a hot wok or skillet, heat the vegetable oil and saut√© the dried red chilies and Sichuan peppercorns until fragrant.
  3. Add the chicken to the pan and stir-fry until fully cooked.
  4. Add garlic, ginger, and green onions, continuing to stir-fry until aromatic.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix the remaining light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, black vinegar, and sugar. Pour this over the chicken and stir well.
  6. Add the peanuts, and continue cooking for another 2 minutes until everything is well coated with the sauce.
  7. Serve hot over steamed rice.
