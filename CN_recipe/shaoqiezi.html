Braised Eggplant

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 2 medium Eggplants
  • 2 cloves Garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp Ginger, minced
  • 2 tbsp Light soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp Oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Chicken or vegetable broth
  • 1 tsp Cornstarch (mixed with 2 tbsp water)
  • 1-2 tbsp Vegetable oil

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Wash the eggplants and cut them into 2-inch long segments. If you're using the long, thin variety, you can simply halve them lengthwise.
  2. In a wok or large skillet, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. Add the eggplant pieces and stir-fry until they begin to soften and turn golden brown. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same wok, add a bit more oil if necessary, and saut√© the minced ginger and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Return the eggplant to the wok. Add the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and broth. Stir well to coat the eggplant.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium-low, cover, and let the eggplant simmer until it's soft and fully cooked through.
  6. If you want a thicker sauce, add the cornstarch mixture and stir until the sauce thickens.
  7. Add the chili flakes or fresh chili if using. Stir well for another minute.
  8. Transfer to a serving dish, garnish with chopped green onions, and serve warm with rice.
