Mapo Tofu

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Soft tofu
  • Ground pork or beef
  • Sichuan peppercorns
  • Fermented black beans
  • Garlic, minced
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Chili bean paste (Doubanjiang)
  • Chicken or vegetable broth
  • Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch (for slurry)
  • Vegetable oil

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 15min

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add Sichuan peppercorns until fragrant.
  2. Add ground pork or beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in the minced garlic and fermented black beans.
  4. Add the chili bean paste and mix well.
  5. Gently add the tofu, being careful not to break it, and stir to coat with the sauce.
  6. Pour in the broth and bring to a simmer.
  7. Make a cornstarch slurry by mixing cornstarch with a little water and add to the pan to thicken the sauce.
  8. Season with soy sauce to taste.
  9. Transfer to a serving dish and garnish with sliced green onions.
  10. Serve hot with rice.
