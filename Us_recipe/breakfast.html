Breakfast

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Eggs (2 per serving)
  • Bacon strips (3-4 per serving)
  • White or whole wheat bread (2 slices per serving)
  • Butter
  • Milk or orange juice for drink
  • Fresh fruits (like bananas, strawberries, or blueberries)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: difficult
    • Required time: 30min

Steps

  1. Preheat a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add bacon strips to the skillet, cook until crispy, then set aside on paper towels.
  3. In the same skillet, crack the eggs and cook them based on preference: scrambled, fried, or over-easy.
  4. While eggs are cooking, toast the bread slices in a toaster or oven.
  5. Spread butter over the toasted bread slices.
  6. Serve the eggs, bacon, and toast on a plate. Add fresh fruits on the side.
  7. Pour a glass of milk or orange juice to accompany the meal.
