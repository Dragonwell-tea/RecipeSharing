Lobster Rolls

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 pound cooked lobster meat, roughly chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh chives, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon fresh dill, finely chopped (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 4 New England-style hot dog buns (split-top)
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Leaf lettuce for serving (optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine lobster meat, mayonnaise, lemon juice, chives, dill (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix gently until well combined.
  2. Preheat a griddle or large skillet over medium heat. Brush the outer sides of the hot dog buns with melted butter.
  3. Place the buns, buttered side down, on the griddle. Toast until golden brown, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  4. If using lettuce, place a lettuce leaf inside each toasted bun. Divide the lobster mixture among the buns.
  5. Serve immediately with lemon wedges and enjoy your delicious Lobster Roll!
