Hawaiian

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 pre-made pizza dough or crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup diced ham
  • 1/2 cup pineapple chunks (canned or fresh)
  • 1/4 cup sliced red bell pepper (optional)
  • 1/4 cup sliced red onion (optional)
  • 1/4 cup chopped bacon (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano (optional)
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 15min

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C) or according to the pizza dough instructions.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Evenly spread the pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Distribute the diced ham, pineapple chunks, and other toppings (if using) evenly over the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle with dried oregano if desired.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  9. Remove from the oven and allow to cool slightly. If desired, garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
  10. Slice and enjoy your Hawaiian Pizza!
