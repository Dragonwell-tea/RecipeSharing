Crab Cakes

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 pound lump crabmeat, picked over for shells
  • 1/3 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon Old Bay seasoning
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter or vegetable oil, for frying
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine crabmeat, breadcrumbs, mayonnaise, egg, Dijon mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, Old Bay seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  2. Gently fold the mixture together until just combined, being careful not to break up the crabmeat.
  3. Shape the mixture into patties, about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Heat butter or oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  5. Once hot, add the crab cakes to the skillet. Cook until golden brown and crispy, about 4-5 minutes on each side.
  6. Transfer the crab cakes to a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.
  7. Serve hot with lemon wedges on the side.
