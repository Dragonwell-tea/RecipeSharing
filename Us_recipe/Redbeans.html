Red Beans&Rice

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 cup dried red beans, soaked overnight
  • 2 cups white rice
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 celery stalks, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper (adjust to taste)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Rinse the soaked red beans under cold water and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, saut√© onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic until softened.
  3. Add the sliced sausage to the pot and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add the rinsed red beans, bay leaf, thyme, paprika, and cayenne pepper. Stir to combine.
  5. Add chicken broth or water to the pot, ensuring the beans are covered by at least an inch of liquid.
  6. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let simmer for about 1.5-2 hours, or until beans are tender. Stir occasionally and add more water if needed.
  7. While beans are simmering, cook rice according to package instructions.
  8. Once beans are tender, season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove the bay leaf.
  9. Serve the red beans over rice, garnished with sliced green onions. Add hot sauce if desired.
