Tteokbokki

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 300g rice cakes (tteok)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 2 tablespoons gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon gochugaru (Korean red chili flakes)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 15min

Steps

  1. Soak rice cakes in warm water for about 15 minutes to soften them if they are refrigerated.
  2. In a pan, add water, gochujang, soy sauce, sugar, garlic, and gochugaru. Mix well and bring it to a boil.
  3. Add the soaked rice cakes and sliced onions to the pan. Stir occasionally and let it simmer for about 10 minutes.
  4. Once the sauce thickens and the rice cakes become soft, add the fish cakes and boiled eggs (if you're using them).
  5. Continue to simmer until the fish cakes are heated through and the sauce is thick and glossy.
  6. Turn off the heat, drizzle with sesame oil, and sprinkle sliced green onions and sesame seeds on top for garnish.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy your homemade Tteokbokki!
