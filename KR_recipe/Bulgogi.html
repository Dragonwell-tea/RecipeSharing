Bulgogi

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 lb thinly sliced beef (ribeye or sirloin)
  • 1 medium yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 1 carrot, julienned (optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine all the marinade ingredients and mix well.
  2. Add the thinly sliced beef to the marinade ensuring each slice is well coated. Allow the meat to marinate for at least 1 hour, preferably longer (overnight is best).
  3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, add the marinated beef slices in a single layer. Do not overcrowd the pan. Cook until the edges of the beef start to brown, then flip to cook the other side. This should only take a couple of minutes per side.
  4. Once the beef is cooked, push it to one side of the pan and add the sliced onions and julienned carrots. Saut√© the vegetables until they are slightly softened and have a bit of color.
  5. Mix the beef and vegetables together in the pan. Cook for an additional 2 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and garnish with chopped green onions and additional sesame seeds.
  7. Serve Bulgogi over steamed rice or wrapped in lettuce leaves.
