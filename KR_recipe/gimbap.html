gimbap

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 2 cups short-grain white rice, cooked
  • 5 sheets of gim (dried seaweed/laver)
  • 1 small carrot, julienned
  • 1/2 cucumber, julienned
  • 4-5 strips of yellow pickled radish (danmuji)
  • 4-5 strips of fried eggs, cut lengthwise
  • 4-5 strips of cooked and seasoned spinach
  • 4-5 slices of imitation crab meat or ham
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Sesame seeds (optional)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Prepare the rice: Mix the cooked rice with sesame oil and a pinch of salt. Set aside to cool down a bit.
  2. Place a gim sheet on a bamboo rolling mat (makisu). Spread a thin layer of rice evenly over the seaweed, leaving about an inch at the top without rice.
  3. On the rice, place a strip of carrot, cucumber, yellow radish, egg, spinach, and crab meat or ham, horizontally.
  4. Starting from the bottom, roll the gim tightly using the bamboo mat, pressing firmly as you roll to ensure the roll stays together.
  5. Once rolled, set aside the finished roll and repeat with the remaining ingredients.
  6. When all the rolls are ready, lightly brush them with sesame oil and sprinkle with sesame seeds (if desired).
  7. Using a sharp knife, slice the rolls into bite-sized pieces. Make sure to wipe the knife with a wet cloth between slices for cleaner cuts.
  8. Serve with soy sauce for dipping or enjoy as is!
