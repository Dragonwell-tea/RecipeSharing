Galbitang

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 lb ground pork (optional)
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup ketchup
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano

    Other

    • Difficulty level: simple
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, soak breadcrumbs in milk for about 5 minutes or until softened.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork (if using), breadcrumb mixture, chopped onion, minced garlic, eggs, ketchup, Worcestershire sauce, thyme, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined but do not overmix to avoid a dense meatloaf.
  4. Shape the mixture into a loaf and place it in a baking dish or on a lined baking tray.
  5. For the glaze, in a separate bowl, mix together ketchup, brown sugar, and Worcestershire sauce. Spread this mixture over the top of the meatloaf.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 1 hour or until the meatloaf is fully cooked and no longer pink in the middle.
  7. Remove from the oven and let it rest for about 10 minutes before slicing.
  8. Enjoy your delicious Meatloaf!
