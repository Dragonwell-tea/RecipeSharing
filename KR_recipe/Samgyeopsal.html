Samgyeopsal

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Pork belly slices (samgyeopsal)
  • Fresh lettuce leaves (for wraps)
  • Thinly sliced garlic
  • Green chili peppers, sliced
  • Kimchi
  • Sesame oil with salt and pepper (for dipping)
  • Ssamjang (Korean dipping sauce, a mix of doenjang and gochujang)
  • Optional: Sliced onions, mushrooms, or other vegetables for grilling

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Heat up the grill (preferably a Korean BBQ grill but any grill or large skillet will do).
  2. Once the grill is hot, place the pork belly slices onto the grill. Allow them to cook until theyâ€™re golden brown and slightly crispy.
  3. Flip the meat occasionally to ensure even cooking. During this time, you can also grill the garlic, chilies, onions, mushrooms, or any other veggies you like.
  4. Once cooked, cut the meat into bite-sized pieces directly on the grill using scissors.
  5. To eat, take a lettuce leaf, add a piece of meat, a dab of ssamjang, some garlic, and other accompaniments. Wrap it up and enjoy in one bite!
  6. Alternatively, you can dip the grilled pork belly in the sesame oil mixture before placing it in the lettuce wrap.
