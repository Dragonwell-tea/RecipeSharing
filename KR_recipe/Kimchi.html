Kimichi

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked white rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons kimchi juice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or sesame oil
  • 1/2 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup diced spam or cooked pork (optional)
  • 2 green onions, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean red pepper paste, optional)
  • Fried egg (for topping)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: difficult
    • Required time: 30min

Steps

  1. Heat the oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add the diced onion and garlic, and saut√© until they become translucent.
  3. If using spam or pork, add it now and fry until lightly browned.
  4. Add the chopped kimchi to the pan and fry for a couple of minutes until it's heated through.
  5. Stir in the kimchi juice, soy sauce, sugar, and gochujang (if using). Mix well.
  6. Add the cooked rice to the pan. Break up any clumps and stir well to ensure the rice is fully coated with the kimchi mixture.
  7. Fry the mixture for a few minutes until the rice becomes slightly crispy.
  8. Stir in the sliced green onions.
  9. Serve the kimchi fried rice in bowls. Top each serving with a fried egg and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
