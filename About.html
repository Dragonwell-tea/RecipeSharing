About this site

Currently, the layout style of the web page looks simple at the moment, and doesn't have a lot of complex layout modules and too much information.

In the development of my website, for the left and right layout of some different modules on the webpage, I used a lot of floats to solve it, relative positioning and absolute positioning I did not use too much, as I am also learning while trying to make this website, so in the subsequent several webpages of the layout design style, I began to try the use of the layout of the heart, I found that the design of the heart of the layout of the page layout is a better way, I'm slowly trying to make my page layout coordinated and beautiful. I am slowly trying to make my page layouts coordinated and aesthetically pleasing.

I'm also trying to find some CSS rules to make some of my modules look good, such as when the mouse hovers over the navigation bar in the header, the background of the font and some changes in color. For layouts such as vertical centering, I tried to use CSS3 rules such as flexible boxes, which I found made it easier to handle some layouts.

I didn't use Javascript too much, but only implemented form integrity checking and popups on a few pages, such as the login page, the registration page, and the share menu module on the personal page.

I will slowly improve this site in the future!
