Curry[カレーライス]

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Rice: 2 cups
  • Japanese curry roux: 1 block
  • Water: 4 cups
  • Chicken thighs or beef: 300 grams, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • Onion: 1, finely chopped
  • Carrots: 2, diced
  • Potatoes: 2, diced
  • Oil: 2 tablespoons
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 15min

Steps

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and cook it according to package instructions.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over medium heat. Add onions and sauté until translucent.
  3. Add the chicken thighs or beef and cook until lightly browned.
  4. Add carrots and potatoes to the pot. Sauté for a few minutes.
  5. Pour in the water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to low and let it simmer until the vegetables are tender.
  6. Break the curry roux block into pieces and add it to the pot. Stir well until the roux is completely dissolved.
  7. Season with salt and pepper as needed. Simmer for an additional 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Once the curry has thickened to your liking, remove from heat.
  9. Serve the curry over cooked rice and enjoy!
