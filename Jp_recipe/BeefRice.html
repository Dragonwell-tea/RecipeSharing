牛丼(BeefRice)

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Thinly sliced beef - 200g
  • Onion - 1, thinly sliced
  • Ginger - a small piece, thinly sliced
  • Rice - 2 bowls
  • Japanese soy sauce - 2 tablespoons
  • Mirin - 2 tablespoons
  • Sake or cooking wine - 1 tablespoon
  • Sugar - 1.5 tablespoons
  • Eggs - 2 (optional)
  • Green onions - 1 stalk, thinly sliced (for garnishing)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: medium
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. In a skillet, add a little oil and sauté the onions and ginger until translucent.
  2. Add the beef slices and stir-fry until they change color.
  3. Add soy sauce, mirin, sake, and sugar. Cook on low heat until the sauce reduces slightly.
  4. If you like, you can crack the eggs on top, cover the skillet, and steam for a few minutes until the eggs are set.
  5. Pour the beef and sauce over a bowl of rice.
  6. Garnish with green onions.
  7. Enjoy your meal.
