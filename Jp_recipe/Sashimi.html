Sashimi

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Tuna - A popular choice for sashimi
  • Salmon - Another popular choice
  • Yellowtail
  • Shrimp
  • Octopus
  • Wasabi - A green, spicy paste often served with sashimi
  • Soy Sauce - For dipping
  • Ginger - Thinly sliced, pickled ginger often served on the side
  • Seaweed - Optional, for garnish
  • Daikon Radish - Thinly sliced, as a garnish or base

    Other

    • Difficulty level: Easy
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Select and purchase fresh fish or seafood. Ensure they are sashimi-grade.
  2. Chill the fish or seafood in the refrigerator for at least an hour to firm it up for slicing.
  3. Use a specialized sashimi knife, which is very sharp.
  4. Wash the fish or seafood and pat it dry gently.
  5. Deseal, debone, and remove the innards if you haven't bought them prepared.
  6. Depending on the type of fish, slice it diagonally, following its grain into thin slices.
  7. Arrange neatly on a plate, often laid over thinly sliced scallions or daikon radish.
  8. Serve with wasabi, soy sauce, and pickled ginger.
  9. When eating, lightly dip into the soy sauce. Be careful not to over-dip as it can overpower the taste of the fish.
