OctopusBalls

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Fresh octopus
  • Flour or specialized takoyaki flour
  • Eggs
  • Green onions, finely chopped
  • Red pickled ginger (optional)
  • Tempura scraps
  • Japanese soy sauce
  • Dashi broth or water
  • Takoyaki sauce
  • Japanese mayonnaise
  • Cooking oil
  • Large seaweed sheets (chopped)
  • Bonito flakes (Katsuobushi)

    Other

    • Difficulty level: difficult
    • Required time: 30min

Steps

  1. Mix eggs, flour, and dashi or water in a large bowl until the batter is smooth.
  2. Add chopped green onions and tempura scraps.
  3. Preheat some cooking oil on a takoyaki pan.
  4. Pour the batter into each hole, filling it about 3/4 full.
  5. Place a piece of octopus in the center of each hole.
  6. When the batter begins to set, use a bamboo skewer to turn it, forming a spherical shape.
  7. Flip the takoyaki ensuring each side is cooked until golden brown.
  8. Place the cooked takoyaki on a plate, drizzle with takoyaki sauce and Japanese mayonnaise.
  9. Sprinkle with seaweed and bonito flakes, adding pickled red ginger if available.
  10. Now, enjoy your delicious takoyaki!
