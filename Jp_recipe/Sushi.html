Sushi

Required ingredients

    List of ingredients

  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori (Seaweed Sheets)
  • Sashimi (Raw Fish Slices)
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Wasabi
  • Soy Sauce

    Other

    • Difficulty level: simple
    • Required time: 20min

Steps

  1. Prepare all the ingredients.
  2. Spread a layer of sushi rice on the nori.
  3. Place the vegetables and sashimi on the rice.
  4. Roll the sushi using a bamboo mat, applying gentle pressure to ensure it's tight.
  5. Using a sharp knife, cut the sushi into appropriate sized pieces.
  6. Serve with a small dish of soy sauce and wasabi.
  7. Enjoy your sushi!
